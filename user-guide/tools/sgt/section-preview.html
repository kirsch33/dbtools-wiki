SGT Section Preview

The SGT section preview shows a cross-section of the wall at the position indicated by the section marker on the elevation preview.

Section Marker

The section marker is the blue dashed vertical line on the elevation preview. Drag it to change the cut position.

  • Snap increments: The marker snaps to 6-inch increments along the wall length.
  • Direction arrows: Filled triangles at the top and bottom of the marker point screen-left, indicating the viewing direction (from exterior to interior).

Viewing Direction

The section view renders the wall cross-section as seen from the exterior side looking toward the interior:

  • Screen-left = exterior face
  • Screen-right = interior face

Far Clip Offset

Only elements whose extent along the wall intersects the clip range around the section cut position are displayed. This behaves similarly to Revit's "Far Clip Offset" for section views.

The clip range is shown as a translucent blue band on the elevation preview, bounded by a dashed line at the left edge only (viewing direction side).

  • Far clip distance: 2'-0"
  • Visible range: from 2'-0" left of the section marker up to the section marker
  • Labeling: no clip distance label is shown on the canvas

Elements (girts, headers, sills) that fall entirely outside this range are hidden in the section view.
